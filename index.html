Kitty Run!

Escape bath time and make as big a mess as you can

Here are the super simple rules

How to play

There are only 2 controls to keep in mind

You can use a short jump with the 'W' key You can use a long jump with the 'SPACE' key

Jump onto and destroy all these items to gain points

Avoid these playful items that slow you down and let your owner catch up
